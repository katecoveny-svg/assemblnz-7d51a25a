## Common questions and preferred answers

### "What does assembl do?"
assembl gives New Zealand businesses specialist operational workflows that reduce admin, surface risk earlier, and keep people in control. It's not a chatbot — it's a governed intelligence layer that connects your real operational data to the right specialist agent for each task.

### "Is it just AI chat?"
No. Chat is the entry point, not the product. Behind every conversation is a governed routing pipeline, specialist agents with domain-specific knowledge, compliance layers, shared business memory, and an audit trail. The agents act, not just advise.

### "What's a kete?"
A kete is an industry pack — a curated set of specialist agents, workflows, templates, and policy guidance tuned for a specific domain. Think of it as an AI-staffed department that already knows your industry's legislation and best practice.

### "Which kete do I need?"
Depends on your industry:
- Running a café, bar, hotel, or tourism operation → **Manaaki** (Hospitality)
- Building houses, commercial construction, civil works → **Waihanga** (Construction)
- Marketing, content creation, brand work → **Auaha** (Creative)
- Running a workshop, fleet, or vehicle dealership → **Arataki** (Automotive)
- Freight, customs, logistics, importing/exporting → **Pikau** (Freight & Customs)

If your business spans more than one domain, the Leader or Enterprise tier gives you access to 2 or all 5 kete.

### "How does the compliance layer work?"
Every output passes through the tikanga compliance pipeline before it reaches you:
1. **Kahu** — checks legislative references and flags legal risk
2. **Tā** — ensures cultural safety and tikanga alignment
3. **Mahara** — checks for privacy risk and PII exposure
4. **Mana** — final quality gate before output is released

This runs on every generation, automatically.

### "Is my data safe?"
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Customer business data is never used to train AI models
- NZ Privacy Act 2020 compliant (including IPP 3A from 1 May 2026)
- Enterprise customers get attested NZ data residency — your data stays in New Zealand
- NZISM-informed security practices
- Full audit trail on every agent output

### "How does onboarding work?"
After signup, assembl runs a structured discovery session to map your business context into shared business memory. This means agents start with knowledge about your business from day one — not from scratch. Setup takes 1–2 weeks depending on tier.

### "Can I try before buying?"
Yes. Book a demo at assembl.co.nz or chat here to explore specific use cases. There's also a free preview for new visitors.

### "Do I need to be technical to use this?"
No. The interface is natural language. Your team asks questions, issues instructions, and reviews outputs — the technical complexity is behind the scenes.

### "Is assembl NZ-built?"
Yes. assembl is built in Auckland. NZ legislation, NZ spelling, NZ data residency options, and tikanga Māori as a structural governance layer — not a feature added after the fact.
