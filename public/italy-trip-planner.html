💡 NZ to Italy Money Tips
Currency: Euro (EUR). As of early 2026, 1 NZD ~ 0.52 EUR. Always check xe.com before you go.

Cards: Wise multi-currency card is the best option from NZ — no foreign exchange fees. Apply 4+ weeks before travel.

Cash: Carry ~200 EUR for small towns and markets. Italy is more cash-friendly than you'd think.

Tipping: Not expected in Italy. "Coperto" (cover charge of 1-3 EUR) is normal at restaurants — that's your tip.

🌎 NZ Citizen Visa Info
Visa-free for 90 days. NZ passport holders can travel to Italy (Schengen Area) for up to 90 days within any 180-day period without a visa.

ETIAS Required. The European Travel Information and Authorisation System is now required for NZ citizens. Apply online before travel — it's EUR 7, valid for 3 years.

Passport: Must be valid for at least 3 months beyond your planned departure from the Schengen area.
✈️ Flight Routes from NZ
No direct flights from NZ to Italy. Common routes:

• Auckland → Dubai → Rome/Milan (Emirates) — ~24-26hrs
• Auckland → Singapore → Rome/Milan (Singapore Airlines) — ~24-27hrs
• Auckland → Doha → Rome (Qatar Airways) — ~26-28hrs
• Auckland → Hong Kong → Rome (Cathay Pacific) — ~25-28hrs

Tip: Book 3-5 months ahead for best prices. Shoulder season (April-May, Sep-Oct) flights are cheaper and Italy is glorious.
