## Act overview

**Full title:** Fair Trading Act 1986 (FTA)
**Administered by:** Commerce Commission
**Relevant to:** AUAHA (creative), ARATAKI (business), and any claim Assembl makes publicly.

---

## Key provisions relevant to Assembl

### Misleading conduct (s9 FTA)

No person shall, in trade, engage in conduct that is misleading or deceptive or is likely to mislead or deceive.

**Relevance to Assembl:** All public claims — including claims about the simulator, evidence pack capabilities, and NZ law compliance — must be assessed against the misleading conduct standard.
