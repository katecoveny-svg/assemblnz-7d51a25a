## Act overview

**Full title:** Privacy Act 2020
**Commencement:** 1 December 2020 (except IPP3A — see below)
**Administered by:** Office of the Privacy Commissioner (OPC)
**Applies to:** any agency (person, body, public or private) that holds personal information about individuals in New Zealand.

---

## Key definitions

- **Personal information:** information about an identifiable individual.
- **Agency:** any person or body who holds personal information (with limited exceptions for individuals acting in a personal capacity).
- **Interference with privacy:** a breach of one of the IPPs that causes harm to an individual.
